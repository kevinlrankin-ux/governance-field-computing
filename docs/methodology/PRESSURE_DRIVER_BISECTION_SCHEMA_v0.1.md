# Pressure Driver Bisection Schema v0.1

## Purpose

Pressure Driver Bisection is the method for taking one driver beneath a compressed governance phrase and asking what must be true for that driver to exist, matter, and generate governance pressure.

## Starting test case

Compressed governance phrase:

> Brush your teeth twice a day.

Immediate driver:

> bacteria

## Required interrogation fields

Each driver must be interrogated through:

- who
- what
- when
- where
- how
- why
- why not
- under what conditions
- with what consequences
- with what evidence strength
- with what ambiguity
- with what discovery value

## Acceptance rule

A driver bisection is not accepted until every claim is either:

1. evidenced,
2. marked as hypothesis,
3. marked as unknown,
4. marked as outside current scope.
